FAQ (Tourism services)

COVID 19 Response

In the current situation of COVID-19, safety of our customers is the highest priority. We assure that appropriate social distancing measures are into practice.

  • The tour bus shall not have more than 20 passengers.
  • Tickets at the hotspot will be bought by a single represenatative to avoid overcrowding.
  • Sightseeing would be implemented in batches.
  • Each hotel room will accomodate less than 3 members.
  • Use of sanitizers,gloves and masks will be emphasized
  • Gyms and Swimming pools will have limited access during rush hours.

Climate Response

The next few months are the peaks of summer in UAE

  • Facilities:
  • Water and cooling refreshments will be provided regularly.
  • Air-conditioned buses and hotel rooms.
  • Recommendations:
  • Tourists are requested to carry an umbrella for outdoor Sightseeing.
  • Use of sun block is recommended.
  • Use of light and comfortable clothing.

Limited Workforce

Due to the current situation of COVID-19 work from home is prefered,therefore resulting in limited workforce

  • UAE has implemented E-Services in all major sectors.
  • 24 Hours Support on call.
  • Virtual tour guides will be available.
  • Dial 8009090 for RTA services

Limitations on International Travel

COVID-19 has caused various restrictions and limitations on International travel

  • Availability of airlines from limited countries.
  • Higher fares.
  • Quarantine terms and conditions should be met as per UAE law.
  • COVID-19 examination to all visitors upon arrival.
